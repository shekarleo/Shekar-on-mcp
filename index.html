Model Context Protocol (MCP)

From First Principles to Enterprise AI Architecture

1️⃣ First Principle Thinking

LLMs are intelligent but stateless. They do not know your enterprise systems. The core problem: How can AI securely access structured enterprise data in real time?

MCP solves this by introducing a standardized communication layer between models and tools.

3️⃣ Why MCP Matters

  • Standardized AI integration
  • Tool discoverability
  • Governance & auditability
  • Separation of reasoning and execution
  • Scalable enterprise AI
